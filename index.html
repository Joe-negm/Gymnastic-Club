About Our Academy

Cairo's Home for Young Gymnasts

Founded in Cairo, Gymnastic Club Academy is a premier training center for boys and girls aged 2 to 14. Our state-of-the-art facility combines expert Egyptian coaching with a warm, supportive community where every child can thrive — from their first cartwheel to national-level competition.

🏆

Championship Coaching

Certified coaches with Egyptian & international competition experience.

🛡️

Safety First

Foam pits, modern equipment and spotting techniques keep kids safe.

🏅

Federation Aligned

Training programs follow the Egyptian Gymnastics Federation standards.

❤️

Fun & Confidence

We build character, discipline and lifelong love for the sport.
